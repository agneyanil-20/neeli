ℹ️ About NEELI

About NEELI

NEELI is a location-based paranormal folklore exploration platform inspired by regional ghost legends, environmental mapping and cultural storytelling across South India.

The platform combines:

  • folklore archives
  • geospatial mapping
  • environmental patterns
  • historical locations
  • user-region analysis

to simulate paranormal “activity zones” in an immersive and atmospheric way.

How NEELI Works

NEELI does not claim to scientifically prove the existence of ghosts or supernatural entities.

Instead, the platform uses a combination of:

  • geographic isolation data
  • historical folklore regions
  • cemetery proximity
  • environmental darkness levels
  • low-density rural mapping
  • local cultural legends
  • nighttime activity windows

to generate paranormal probability zones across the map.

These zones are inspired by real folklore stories, reported myths and region-specific supernatural beliefs.

Paranormal Activity Mapping

The system analyzes nearby environmental conditions such as:

  • graveyard locations
  • forest regions
  • abandoned routes
  • isolated roads
  • riversides
  • low-light regions
  • historical folklore hotspots

Using this data, NEELI creates atmospheric “signal regions” where paranormal folklore entities are more likely to appear within the experience.

Higher anomaly regions may contain:

  • stronger folklore entities
  • rare paranormal sightings
  • region-specific spirits
  • historical supernatural records

Regional Folklore Intelligence

NEELI dynamically changes entities depending on geographic region.

Examples:

Kerala:

  • Yakshi
  • Odiyan
  • Gulikan
  • Neeli

Tamil Nadu:

  • Mohini Pey
  • Sudalai Madan
  • Kollivai Pisasu

Karnataka:

  • Bhoota
  • Panjurli
  • Kallurti

The archive system is designed to preserve and present regional folklore traditions in an interactive exploration format.

Scientific & Cultural Approach

NEELI is designed as:

  • a folklore exploration platform
  • a cultural storytelling experience
  • an atmospheric paranormal simulation

The app blends:

  • environmental mapping
  • geolocation systems
  • folklore documentation
  • cinematic interaction design

to create an immersive supernatural investigation experience.

NEELI does not present paranormal entities as verified scientific fact. The platform is intended for entertainment, folklore preservation and atmospheric exploration.
